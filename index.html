AI-Enhanced UI Design

What kind of website is that? The principal aim of this project is to help UX/UI/web design/graphic design professionals and people interested in the field by giving them access to educational materials on the latest AI tools, and trends that can be implemented in design. The platform will feature tutorials, information on the latest trends, and tools that help designers improve their workflows. The structure of the website is designed to have 10 pages that cover various topics related to AI in design.
